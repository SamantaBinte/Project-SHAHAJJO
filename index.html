What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is the crippling feeling you feel before any task. Fear, dread, and unease are all feelings associated with anxiety. It may make you sweat, feel restless and tight, and cause your heart to race. It's a common stress response. When confronted with a challenging situation at work, taking a test, or making a major decision, you may experience anxiety. It can assist you in coping. Anxiety may give you an energy boost or help you concentrate. For persons with anxiety disorders, however, the worry is persistent and can be overwhelming.

What is a Panic Attack?

Panic attacks are feelings of panic that arrive suddenly and without notice. These episodes can happen at any time, even while you're sleeping. People who are having a panic attack may feel they are going insane or dying. During a panic attack, a person's fear and horror are out of proportion to the genuine situation and may be unrelated to what is going on around them.
